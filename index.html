Take a quick check-in to see how you're doing today.

This short check-in assesses three areas:

Mental Health your emotional and cognitive wellbeing
Stress Resilience how well you're handling pressure
Social Cohesion your sense of connection at work

At the end, a color will represent your current state:

Green — Balanced and doing well
Orange — Moderate, with room for care
